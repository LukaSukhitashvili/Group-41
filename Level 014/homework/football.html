About Football

Football, also known as soccer in some countries, is a team sport played between two teams of 11 players each.

Key aspects of football include:

  • Objective: Score goals by getting the ball into the opponent's goal
  • Duration: Two 45-minute halves
  • Field: Rectangular grass or artificial turf
  • Equipment: Soccer ball, goals, and player gear

Football is known for its global popularity and is often called "The Beautiful Game".
