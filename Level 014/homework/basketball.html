About Basketball

Basketball is a team sport played between two teams of five players each.

Key aspects of basketball include:

  • Objective: Score points by shooting the ball through the opponent's hoop
  • Duration: Four 12-minute quarters (NBA)
  • Court: Rectangular hardwood floor
  • Equipment: Basketball, hoop, backboard

Basketball is known for its fast-paced action and high-scoring games.
