It's Not a Diary, It's a Movie

"It's Not a Diary, It's a Movie" is a 2023 German comedy film directed by Anika Decker. The movie follows the life of a young woman named Tilly who, in her quest for self-discovery and love, decides to document her life on camera. The film combines humor with heartfelt moments as Tilly navigates relationships and personal challenges, all while capturing her journey on film.

The title reflects the main character's attempt to make her life as cinematic and meaningful as the movies she adores, offering a blend of comedy, romance, and introspection.
