My Favorite Recipe: Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

For the sauce:
500g ground beef
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
400g canned tomatoes
2 tablespoons tomato paste
1 teaspoon dried oregano
Salt and pepper to taste

For serving:
400g spaghetti
Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

1. Brown the beef: In a large pan, cook the ground beef until browned.
2. Add vegetables: Add onion and garlic, cook until softened.
3. Add tomatoes: Stir in canned tomatoes, tomato paste, and oregano.
4. Simmer: Let the sauce simmer for 30 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Cook pasta: Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
6. Serve: Divide spaghetti among plates, top with sauce and generously sprinkle grated Parmesan.

Enjoy your homemade Spaghetti Bolognese!
