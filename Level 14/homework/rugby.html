About Rugby

Rugby is a contact team sport that originated in England in the first half of the 19th century.

Key aspects of rugby include:

  • Objective: Score more points than the opposing team
  • Duration: Two 40-minute halves
  • Field: Rectangular grass field
  • Equipment: Rugby ball, goal posts

Rugby is known for its physical nature and strategic gameplay.
