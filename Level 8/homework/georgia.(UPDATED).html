HISTORY OF GEORGIA!!!

Georgia, or Sakartvelo, is a country with a rich and ancient history. Situated at the crossroads of Europe and Asia, it has been inhabited since prehistoric times.

The Kingdom of Georgia reached its zenith in the 12th and 13th centuries under Queen Tamar, known for cultural and political achievements. Over centuries, Georgia faced invasions and occupations by various empires, including Persian, Roman, Byzantine, and Ottoman.

In the 19th century, it was annexed by the Russian Empire and later became part of the Soviet Union, gaining independence again in 1991.

Notable Historical Sites in Georgia

Mtskheta - An ancient city and UNESCO World Heritage Site Uplistsikhe - A rock-hewn town dating back to the 1st millennium BC Gelati Monastery - A medieval monastic complex and UNESCO World Heritage Site

Georgian Culture and Traditions

Georgia is known for its unique culture, traditions, and hospitality. Some of the notable aspects include:

Georgian cuisine, featuring dishes like khinkali, khachapuri, and mtsvadi Georgian wine-making tradition, with a history dating back thousands of years Georgian polyphonic singing, inscribed on the UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage list
